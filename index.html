ALEX and KATH



My Lover

For I am blessed
Determined to give my best
You need no guess
Love for you is the purest

Our moments, tested by time
In all universes I plan to make you mine
Endearment and passion of mine, only offered to thine
Our reputation surely will age like wine

As sun shines every morning,
Though mishaps keep on pouring,
Hardships through the evening
Like the eternal flame, our affection will keep on burning

Countless midnight, alone and in sorrow
I cannot fathom the pain each time you go
Hoping that someday, or even tomorrow
I'll have to witness the beauty of you

A woman so brave, strong, and bold
Yet enclosed in a room of solitude
I wrote and offer you this simple ode
For our story will be tailored in fortitude

Time has come, our love be told like lore
‘Fearless I am to ask you now for more,
‘Tis the moment that I prayed for
Would you be with me until forevermore?
